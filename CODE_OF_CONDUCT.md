# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want `datareadme` to be a welcoming, respectful project for everyone.

Contributors, maintainers, and community members are expected to make participation harassment-free for all people, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity or expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and constructive
- Giving helpful, specific feedback
- Assuming good intent while still being honest
- Sharing context when reporting bugs or suggesting changes
- Focusing on what makes the project better for users

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Trolling or deliberate disruption
- Publishing private information without permission
- Dismissing others in a hostile or demeaning way

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards and may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, or other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private security-style report if appropriate or contact the maintainer through GitHub.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in project spaces and in public spaces when someone is representing the project.
